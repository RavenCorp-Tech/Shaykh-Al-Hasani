Al-Albānī's Manhaj Regarding the Hidden Defects of Ḥadīth (ʿIlal al-Ḥadīth)

Format: Video Series
Parts: 2

Summary: A two-part lecture by Shaykh al-Muḥaddith Abū ʿAlī al-Ḥasanī discussing Shaykh al-Albānī’s approach to the science of hidden defects (ʿilal) in ḥadīth. The series clarifies whether Al-Albānī gave due consideration to the defect-identifications of the earlier ḥadīth masters (al-ʾAʾimmah) and presents representative examples where defect-judgments were explicitly stated.
